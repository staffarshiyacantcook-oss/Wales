Welcome to Cardiff

The capital city of Wales, full of history, culture, and amazing places.

Presentation

Cardiff is the largest city in Wales and one of the most interesting places in the UK. It has a mix of old and new, from ancient castles to modern waterfronts. In this project, I will show some of the most important places in Cardiff.

Cardiff Castle

A historic castle in the centre of the city, with over 2,000 years of history.
